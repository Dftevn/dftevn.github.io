Research

Main focus of our group is searching for novel solid-state electrolytes for all-solid-state batteries by using computational methods. Main methods we apply are the evolutionary crystal structure search method USPEX [reference] for searching novel materials, DFT as implemented in VASP and QuantumEspresso software packages [][]. We also employ machine learning models to accelerate our calculations.
Anther area of research are the novel 2D crystal structures. We also investigate novel gas sensing materials by ab-initio methods.
